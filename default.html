TABLE TENNIS CLUB NEWS




Inti Subang College holds "Charity Tournament".

Table Tennis Club in Inti will hold "Charity Table Tennis Tournament". Other students as well as Inti studentscan be allowed to participate in the tournament if they pay RM5 and have their table tennis racket. If you win this tournament, the Table Tennis Club will donate all the money with the name of winner. In addition, we will offer a certificate of winner. We look forward to seeing you in the tournament. If you have any further inquiry, please feel free to send an email to Inti Table Tennis Club.

N.W.T. and Yukon train for table tennis

Ecole Whitehorse Elementary School hold three-day session of table tennis. The team was welcomed by the Yukon Canada Winter Games team to represent the Northwest Regions at the games in what Yukon coach Kevin described. Players and coaches of table tennis from the Yukon and Northwest Territories took a moment from their training to take a picture on weekend at school. Even though the training was not easy, everyone was very satisfied with it.

Table Tennis Networker Award

All young people between 15 and 18 years can receive the table tennis England TT Networker - Table Tennis Leadership Award. There are some aims of the awards. First of all, it is to encourage TT Networkers (Leaders) to organize joyful and purposeful Table Tennis Activities focused on primarily youngsters. Second, it is to provide the motivation into Table Tennis Coaching as well as organizing competitions. There is a small charge for this course unless funding is offered.
